True Level
by Hazer
📦 Description

This mod for ICARUS removes the visual level cap, allowing you to see your actual progress beyond the default limit of Level 60. It accurately tracks and displays your "True Level" based on your earned experience.

Perfect for players who:

want to see their long-term progression and total XP earned

are tired of being stuck at the Level 60 visual cap

want to compare their actual experience levels with friends in multiplayer

⚙️ Features

🔼 Uncapped Level Display: Shows your real level instead of stopping at 60

📊 Accurate XP Tracking: Seamlessly calculates level based on your character's total XP

⚡ No Performance Impact: Lightweight and purely UI-focused

🔧 Easy to install: Simple setup for immediate progression tracking

📥 Installation

Download the mod files

Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:
Icarus\Content\Paks\

Place the mod file there (e.g. .pak file)

Launch the game

❌ Uninstallation

Remove the mod file from the Paks folder

Restart the game

⚠️ Notes

Primarily a UI modification; does not grant additional talent points unless paired with specific mods

Works best if all multiplayer participants have the mod installed

Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates

🔄 Compatibility

Tested with the current version of ICARUS (at time of release)

Should be compatible with most QoL and UI mods
