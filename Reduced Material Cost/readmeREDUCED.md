# Hazers Reduced Material Costs and Speed Up

New Items Included. Iron Ingot, Aluminium, Glass 1- 3 and many more

## 📦 Description

**Hazers Reduced Material Costs and Speed Up** is a mod for **ICARUS** that rebalances crafting by **reducing material requirements** and **significantly speeding up crafting times**.

Now with Wooden & Bone Tools

Created by **Hazer / Shelly3000**, this mod is designed to streamline progression, reduce grind, and make the overall gameplay experience smoother and more enjoyable—without removing the core survival challenge.

Perfect for players who:

* want less grind and faster progression
* prefer a more relaxed or time-efficient experience
* enjoy building and crafting without heavy resource farming

---

## ⚙️ Features

* 🔽 Reduced material costs for many recipes
* ⚡ Faster crafting times across multiple items
* 🛠️ Smoother progression and less downtime
* 🎮 Maintains overall gameplay balance while reducing grind
* 🚀 Ideal for solo and multiplayer sessions

---

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the mod files
2. Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:

   ```
   Icarus\Content\Paks\
   ```
3. Place the mod file there (e.g. `.pak` file)
4. Launch the game

---

## ❌ Uninstallation

1. Remove the mod file from the `Paks` folder
2. Restart the game

---

## ⚠️ Notes

* May conflict with other mods that modify crafting costs or timers
* In multiplayer, it is recommended that all players use the same mod setup
* Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates
