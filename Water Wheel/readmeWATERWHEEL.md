Hazers Place Water Wheel Everywhere
by Hazer
📦 Description

This mod for ICARUS removes the placement restrictions for Water Wheels. It allows you to place them in any body of water, including still water (lakes/ponds), instead of being limited to flowing rivers.

Perfect for players who:

want to build their base away from rivers but still need water power

prefer more freedom in base design and location

want to utilize lakes and ponds for power generation

⚙️ Features

🔼 Anywhere Placement: Place Water Wheels in still water or lakes

🛠️ Full Functionality: Water Wheels will generate power regardless of water flow

⚡ No Performance Impact: Lightweight modification for maximum efficiency

🔧 Easy to install: Simple drag-and-drop installation

📥 Installation

Download the mod files

Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:
Icarus\Content\Paks\

Place the mod file there (e.g. .pak file)

Launch the game

❌ Uninstallation

Remove the mod file from the Paks folder

Restart the game

⚠️ Notes

May be incompatible with other mods that modify Water Wheel placement or power generation

Works best if all multiplayer participants have the mod installed

Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates

🔄 Compatibility

Tested with the current version of ICARUS (at time of release)

Should be compatible with most QoL mods
