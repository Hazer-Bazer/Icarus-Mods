Hazers Low Exposition & Cave Overhaul
by Hazer
📦 Description

This mod for ICARUS significantly tones down the planet's harshest environmental elements. It provides a more forgiving and focused survival experience by scaling back exposure effects and neutralizing cave-related hazards, allowing you to explore the surface and sub-surface without constant debuffs.

Perfect for players who:

want to focus on exploration and building rather than fighting the weather

are tired of being interrupted by cave sickness and pneumonia

prefer crystal-clear visibility across all biomes

want a more relaxed prospecting experience

⚙️ Features

🔼 Environmental Dampening: Severe biome exposures and debuffs reduced by 80%

🦠 Subterranean Neutrality: Cave sickness and underground debuffs completely removed (0%)

👁️ Optical Enhancement: Distance fog scaling removed for unparalleled, clear visibility

🛡️ Hazard Immunity: Neutralizes periodic Damage Over Time (DoT) like poison, bleeding, or burning

⚡ No Performance Impact: Lightweight modification for a smoother experience

📥 Installation

Download the mod files

Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:
Icarus\Content\Paks\

Place the mod file there (e.g. .pak file)

Launch the game

❌ Uninstallation

Remove the mod file from the Paks folder

Restart the game

⚠️ Notes

May be incompatible with other mods that modify weather, exposure, or debuff values

Works best if all multiplayer participants have the mod installed

Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates

🔄 Compatibility

Tested with the current version of ICARUS (at time of release)
