# Hazers Lower Energy Costs

## 📦 Description

**Hazers Lower Energy Costs** is a mod for **ICARUS** that reduces the **power consumption of electrical devices**, making your energy systems more efficient and easier to maintain.

Created by **Hazer / Shelly3000**, this mod is perfect for players who want to spend less time managing power generation and more time building, exploring, and progressing.

Perfect for players who:

* want more efficient energy usage
* are tired of constantly supplying power
* run large or complex electrical setups
* prefer smoother and less restrictive gameplay

---

## ⚙️ Features

* 🔋 Reduced energy consumption for many devices
* ⚡ More efficient power usage across your base
* 🏗️ Supports larger builds with less infrastructure
* 🔌 Less reliance on multiple generators
* 🚀 Balanced quality-of-life improvement

---

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the mod files
2. Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:

   ```
   Icarus\Content\Paks\
   ```
3. Place the mod file there (e.g. `.pak` file)
4. Launch the game

---

## ❌ Uninstallation

1. Remove the mod file from the `Paks` folder
2. Restart the game

---

## ⚠️ Notes

* May conflict with other mods that modify power consumption or energy systems
* In multiplayer, it is recommended that all players use the same mod setup
* Use mods at your own risk, especially after updates

---

## 🔄 Compatibility

* Tested with the current version of ICARUS
* Compatible with most QoL mods unless they alter energy systems
