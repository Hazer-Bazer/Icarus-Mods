# Hazers Higher Energy Production

## 📦 Description

**Hazers Higher Energy Production** is a mod for **ICARUS** that increases the **power output of energy-producing devices**, allowing you to generate significantly more electricity with the same infrastructure.

Created by **Hazer / Shelly3000**, this mod is designed to improve efficiency, reduce the need for excessive generator setups, and support larger, more complex bases without constant power shortages.

Perfect for players who:

* build large or power-intensive bases
* want more efficient energy production
* are tired of managing multiple generators
* use advanced electrical setups and automation

---

## ⚙️ Features

* ⚡ Increased energy output from generators and power sources
* 🔋 More efficient power management
* 🏗️ Supports larger and more complex base builds
* 🔌 Reduces the need for excessive power infrastructure
* 🚀 Smooth and balanced enhancement to gameplay

---

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the mod files
2. Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:

   ```
   Icarus\Content\Paks\
   ```
3. Place the mod file there (e.g. `.pak` file)
4. Launch the game

---

## ❌ Uninstallation

1. Remove the mod file from the `Paks` folder
2. Restart the game

---

## ⚠️ Notes

* May conflict with other mods that modify energy production or power systems
* In multiplayer, it is recommended that all players use the same mod setup
* Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates

---

## 🔄 Compatibility

* Tested with the current version of ICARUS (at time of release)
* Compatible with most Quality-of-Life mods unless they alter power systems
