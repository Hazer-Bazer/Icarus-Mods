Charging Backpacks
by Hazer
📦 Description

This mod for ICARUS enhances your survival capabilities by allowing backpacks to charge electronic items and tools while they are equipped. No more running out of battery during long expeditions – your backpack now acts as a mobile power station for your gear.

Perfect for players who:

frequently use electronic tools and equipment in the field

want to reduce downtime spent at charging stations

go on long-distance missions far away from their main base

⚙️ Features

🔼 Mobile Charging: Equipped backpacks now provide power to electronic items in your inventory

🔋 Stay Powered: Keeps your flashlights, tools, and other electronic gear ready for use

⚡ Seamless Integration: Works with existing backpack mechanics

🔧 Easy to install: Simple setup for immediate utility

📥 Installation

Download the mod files

Navigate to your ICARUS installation folder:
Icarus\Content\Paks\

Place the mod file there (e.g. .pak file)

Launch the game

❌ Uninstallation

Remove the mod file from the Paks folder

Restart the game

⚠️ Notes

May be incompatible with other mods that modify backpack attributes or power systems

Works best if all multiplayer participants have the mod installed

Use mods at your own risk, especially after game updates

🔄 Compatibility

Tested with the current version of ICARUS (at time of release)

Should be compatible with most QoL and armor mods
